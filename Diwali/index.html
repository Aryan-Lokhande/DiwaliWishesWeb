Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is a joyous and significant Hindu festival celebrated by millions of people around the world. The festival symbolizes the triumph of light over darkness and good over evil. It brings families and communities together, with homes illuminated by oil lamps and colorful decorations. People exchange gifts, enjoy delicious sweets, and burst fireworks to mark the occasion. Beyond the celebrations, Diwali conveys a message of hope, unity, and the renewal of life. It is a time for reflection, gratitude, and spreading happiness to all.
